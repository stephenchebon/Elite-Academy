Play Ground

Once in a while, we do have organized picnics, and trips within Nairobi and its environments. These trips are enjoyable to most of our learners as they get the time to play and social.

Music and Dance

We do have dance and music clubs where the learners get to share and learner different dancing moves in addition to learning basics about chnaging musics trends

Arts and Crafts

We offer art and craft lessons in addition to the huge selection of fun and easy craft ideas for kids of all ages. Lots of our crafts make use of items that can be found online

Safe Transportation

During our field trips or picnics, we do use safe traspotation buses which are Buckle up and have seat belts are available. When kids stay in their seats, they are able to keep their head, arms and papers inside the bus, and talk quietly. Keeping the aisles clear of books and bags.

Healthy food

You don't need to worry about healthy food as your learners take their food from home but when we meet for trps or a get together, our School offers healthy food practises. we also take them through some of the healthy food habits given that this is the perfect age and time for children to learn about healthy food, bodies and activity.
